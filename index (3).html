Our Story

Built for people who take their burgers seriously.

The High Joint started with one idea: Chennai deserved a burger joint that didn't cut corners. Every patty is pressed fresh, every piece of chicken is fried to order, and every sauce is made in-house — nothing off a shelf.

What began as a single outlet has grown into a city-wide favourite, now serving loaded burgers, wings, and fries across seven neighbourhoods — from Anna Nagar to Tambaram — while keeping the same laid-back, no-fuss energy that got people hooked in the first place.

Fresh, never frozen

Patties are hand-pressed daily at every outlet — not shipped in pre-formed.

House-made sauces

Every glaze, aioli and dip is made in-house — no bottled shortcuts.

7 outlets, one standard

Same recipe, same quality check, no matter which branch you walk into.
